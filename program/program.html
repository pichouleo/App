First training

For the first workout of the week, we will
work on different areas of the upper
body: pectorals, biceps & triceps, back
muscles and abs & oblique.

Second training

For the second training of the week, we will
work mainly on the back of the upper
body: deltoids, trapezius, lumbar
and finally abs & oblique.

Third training

For the third workout of the week, it will
be similar to the first but in a different
order: dorsals, biceps & triceps,
pecs and abs & obliques.

This program is only for the upper body.
